Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how EV World uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website. It explains what these technologies are and why we use them, as well as your rights to control our use of them.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used by website owners to make their websites work, or to work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information.

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off in our systems.

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to track visitors across websites to display relevant advertisements.

Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Session Cookies Maintain your session while browsing our website Session
Preference Cookies Remember your preferences and settings 1 year
Security Cookies Protect your account and data Session
Analytics Cookies Help us understand how visitors use our website 2 years

How to Control Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. If you do this, however, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit a site and some services and functionalities may not work.

  • You can set your browser to refuse all or some browser cookies
  • You can set your browser to alert you when websites set or access cookies
  • You can delete cookies that have already been set
  • You can use our cookie consent tool to manage your preferences
